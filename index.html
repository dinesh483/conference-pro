About The Conference

Scope of the Conference: The International Conference on Advanced Materials for Technological Applications (SUSTAINMAT-2027) covers multidisciplinary research in biomedical materials, sustainable materials, nanotechnology, renewable energy, advanced manufacturing, and emerging technologies.

It provides an international platform for students, scientists, engineers, researchers, and industry professionals to exchange knowledge, present research findings, and explore innovative applications of advanced materials. The conference encourages novel solutions for sustainable development, materials circularity, and low-carbon industrial transformation.

The event promotes the deployment of cutting-edge materials for multidisciplinary technological applications, while encouraging effective technology transfer between academia and industry. SUSTAINMAT-2027 aims to be a dynamic platform for disseminating knowledge in the listed thematic areas.

Author Guidelines

Submissions must be original, unpublished work and should follow the conference template. Full papers are reviewed in a double-blind process; please remove author names and affiliations from the manuscript for the initial review stage.

Paper page limit:

Regular papers: up to 8 pages including figures and references. Extra pages may be allowed with additional charges as per the registration policy.

Formatting:
  • Use the IEEE two-column format (US Letter) unless otherwise specified.
  • Embed figures at high resolution; vector graphics are preferred for line art.
  • References should follow IEEE citation style consistently.

For detailed submission steps and policy, see the Call for Papers page. Sample files and templates are available from the downloads area.
