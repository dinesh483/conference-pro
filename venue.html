Explore K.S.Rangasamy College of Technology

Getting Here

By Air

Nearest Airport: Coimbatore International Airport (CJB).

Distance: 90 km. Taxi services are available directly to the campus (Approx. 2 hours).

By Train

Nearest Station: Erode Junction (ED).

Distance: 15 km. Major trains from Chennai, Bangalore, and Kerala stop here.

By Road

Bus Stop: KSR Kalvi Nagar (On Erode-Tiruchengode Hwy).

Frequent buses available from Erode Bus Stand (Number 10, 12 series).
