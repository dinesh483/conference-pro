Visa

International participants traveling to India for the conference generally require a Conference Visa. Please start the process at least 6–8 weeks before the event.

General Requirements

Visa requirements can vary by nationality and consulate. Always verify the latest rules with your nearest Indian embassy/consulate and the official e‑Visa portal.

Official Indian Visa Online Portal

Requesting an Invitation Letter

An official invitation letter is issued only to registered participants. To request one:

  1. Complete the full registration and payment process.
  2. Email the conference team with the subject “Visa Invitation Request – [Your Name]”.
  3. Attach a scanned copy of your passport (bio‑data page).
  4. Include your Paper ID (authors) or Registration ID (listeners).

Required Documents (Typical)

While requirements may vary by country, you will typically need:

  • Passport with at least 6 months validity.
  • Copy of the official invitation letter.
  • Recent passport‑size photographs (as per consulate guidelines).
  • Additional supporting documents as requested by your embassy/consulate.
Disclaimer: The conference organizers can provide supporting letters (where applicable), but the final decision on visa issuance is made solely by the respective embassy/consulate.
